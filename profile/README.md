## 🔬 Acadify AI Labs & Focus Areas

We specialize in complex AI engineering, product development, and developer tooling. Our primary focus areas are:

### 🤖 1. Enterprise AI & LLM Integrations
* **Custom LLM Fine-Tuning & LoRA/QLoRA:** Adapting open-source and proprietary models for domain-specific operations.
* **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG):** Advanced multi-modal knowledge retrieval with low latency.
* **LLM Security Gateway & Proxies:** PII redaction, prompt injection filtering, and secure enterprise routing.

### 🚀 2. Product Engineering & MVP Development
* **Rapid-to-Market MVPs:** Turning concepts into validated, premium-grade SaaS applications.
* **High-Scale Web & Mobile Architectures:** Building robust frontends and scalable distributed backends.
* **SLA-Backed Systems:** Resilient cloud architecture with strict uptime and performance metrics.

### 🛡️ 3. AI Safety, Evaluation & Quality Assurance
* **AI Model Evaluation Frameworks:** Validating LLM outputs for hallucinations, semantic alignment, and biases.
* **AI Reliability Testing:** Stress-testing model behaviors under adversarial payloads and continuous integration.
